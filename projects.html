Robot in Three Days 2021

In three days, we revised our robot from Ri3D 2020 to better acomplish the 2021 challenge, which was similar to 2020's challenge due to COVID-19. I led the build team to fix our ball intake system, repair the pnumatics, build and mount a mechanism to shoot balls, and install a gyroscope. We worked slowly because of the additional distancing and sanitation requirements imposed by COVID, as well as a significantly smaller team spread over multiple locations across Raliegh, and even across the state. We worked hard to effectively communicate between workshops, utilizing walkie-talkies and a remote CAD team to help each build site understand how different components would fit together on the final robot. Our biggest challenge was designing components to fit together properly, as everything fabricated at the remote sites couldn't be brought to the main space until the last day.
